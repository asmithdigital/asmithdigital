Degree Finder UX
University of Adelaide

The Degree finder application is custom built by the internal web development team at the University of Adelaide.

This application is used by thousands of people during their research process. This includes potential and current students, parents of students, and many others.

My job was to understand the user behaviour, expectations and needs. I conducted user interviews with contextual enquiry, then analysed the data using affinity diagramming techniques.

The data extracted during the analysis stage was used to create user stories, red routes, user requirements and Information architecture requirements.

We then iterated the design with wireframes, and paper prototypes, to improve the user centered design solution.
